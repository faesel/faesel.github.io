10:T2a6a,<p>I&#39;ve been using C# for about a decade now, and every now and again I discover something that surprises me. This week it&#39;s the ability to deconstruct as we do in Javascript (and I&#39;m not talking about using Tuples!).</p>
<p>Below is a simple example of deconstruction taking place to draw out the power, and defence property for our Trex object,</p>

</blockquote>
